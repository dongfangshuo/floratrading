New Generation of Elantra from Beijing Hyundai
Published:2012-11-14 11:12:44    Text Size:【BIG】【MEDIUM】【SMALL】
Summary:
Beijing Hyundai officially begun selling the newest version of the Elantra in China.The new Elantra, which is being sold under the Chinese name Langdong, is priced between 105,800 and 149,800 yuan ($16,731-$23,690). This newest Elantra will be sold in the country alongside the previous two generations.
